About Us
PERC was founded in 2001 and began it's journey from a small office at Liberty Heights, Gulberg, Lahore, Pakistan. In a short span of 13 years the company signed a myriad of contracts with not only Local but also National businesses. By 2014, PERC had created branch offices all over Pakistan; with it's headquarters in Lahore. It had also formed three business units: PERC, Thermatec and Fibrwrx. PERC's mission is to be a leader in all the fields in which it operates and it aims to accomplish this goal by following a set of core values:
  1. Customer Satisfaction: PERC honors it's commitments and strives to meet customer expectations.
  2. Teamwork: PERC employees repect and trust each other, share knowledge and work cohesively to achieve company targets.
  3. Excellence: PERC aims for high standards and continuously strives to improve quality of services.
  4. Leadership: PERC inspires initiative and inspires others to achieve positive result
  5. Rewarding Environment: PERC respects the individual, invests in employee development and rewards success
